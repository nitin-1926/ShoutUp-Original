About ShoutUp

ShoutUp is a non-profit organisation working for the welfare of the women since 2017. We have succesfully helped over 100 women around India.

We provide an enviornment which feels like home. We conduct number of self-help sessions for women. We help them in discovering their potential by providing them classes on cooking, designing, embroidery, engineering and so on.

  • Safe and Friendly Enviornment
  • Education and Training Facilities
  • Employment Opportunities
  • Full Confidentiality throughout the Process

Success Stories

“Roia Atmar has a fulfilling job with the ShoutUp Website which provides emergency accommodation and support to women and children. Almost 20 years ago, Roia was hospitalised for three months after her then husband doused her in turpentine and set her on fire. She suffered horrific injuries – but it was in the hospital that she realised for the first time she could leave her abuser, thanks to the vigilance of this website. She says she received excellent support and that it is possible to find happiness after abuse.”

“Faliana Lee is the author of Carving A Piece of Heaven, which documents her leaving story after almost two decades of being physically and emotionally abused. Lee now works voluntarily with the ShoutUp, to lobby the government for policies to speed up recovery for victims.It became possible only because of ShoutUp website which led the Faliana in the right direction and gave her support.”

“Kim Gentle runs horse workshops for troubled Indigenous youth, where she teaches them to care for and interact with the animals, and learn to give and receive trust. She is now expanding the program and hopes to bring adults with addiction problems into the program. She began the horse therapy clinics after leaving a violent relationship with the help of ShoutUp.”
